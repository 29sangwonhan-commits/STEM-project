About Me

Hello! I'm Han Sangwon, a dedicated web app developer with a passion for building elegant, efficient, and user-friendly digital solutions. My expertise lies in harnessing the power of HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to bring innovative ideas to life. I thrive on problem-solving and continually expanding my knowledge to keep pace with the ever-evolving web landscape.

My approach to development is rooted in a minimalist aesthetic, focusing on clean code and intuitive design to ensure a seamless user experience. I believe that powerful applications don't need to be complex in their presentation.
